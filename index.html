Our Materials

Metal. Wood. Stone.

Built to last and designed to impress. We work with the finest materials to ensure every piece is both beautiful and durable.

⚙️
Metal

Structural steel, stainless, and custom alloys. Welded, powder-coated, or polished to perfection. The backbone of every custom piece.

🌲
Wood

Reclaimed, solid, and engineered woods. Oak, walnut, pine, and maple. Warm texture that contrasts beautifully with cold metal.

Natural Stone

Marble, granite, quartz, and onyx. Each slab is unique — no two pieces are ever the same. The ultimate luxury statement.

Custom Finishes
